OBJECTIVES



This virtual lab aims to provide an interactive and hands-on learning platform for students and professionals to explore digital electronics. It allows users to design, simulate, and analyze digital circuits without the need for physical hardware. By offering real-time simulations and guided experiments, the lab enhances conceptual understanding and problem-solving skills.

Key Objectives:

  • Provide a practical and interactive learning experience.

  • Enable virtual design and analysis of digital circuits.

    • Cover fundamental topics such as:

  • Logic gates and Boolean algebra

  • Combinational and sequential circuits

  • Flip-flops, counters, and registers

  • Offer real-time simulations for better clarity.

  • Bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical implementation.
